Background

What is Apres? Apres (Ah-pr-eh) is a French term for the word 'after' and is in refrence to the famous fairy tale lines: 'They lived happily ever after.' Apres here is in refrence to the 'After' or to Neverland. It is a missing fairy kingdom that was lost during a war between Apres and two other kingdoms: Kiligian and Alinian. The kingdom of Apres has long since been abondened and left to ruin. Its people are seemingly extinct, however, legend says a few Apresians still roam the remaining two kingdoms...

What is Bloom? Bloom is in refrence to the main character of this story, Rose, who was gifted by the last Apresians with the ablitiy to maniplate plants. It's also in refrence to the mythical wild flowers of Apres that bloom in its ruins.

"Where flowers bloom, so does hope."
