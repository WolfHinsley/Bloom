Welcome to Apres!

This is an interactive fractured fairy tale webpage.

About Bloom

Here, you can read about fairy tale characters and interact with their world as you interact with various aspects of our website. Follow along various characters like Red Riding Hood, Pinocchio, Peter Pan, Cinderella, and Rupunzel in their search for the lost kingdom of Apres. Learn more about characters by going to our stats page.

What You Need to Know:

Many characters found on this website are reimagined versions of fairytale or childrens book characters from various fantasy stories. However, Rose, the main character you'll be interacting with, is not based upon any story, but is representive of the reader. Feel free to look around and look for secerts and refrences as you look through this website.
